### Change Management Principles

```
1. Transparency First
   - Explain WHY before WHAT
   - Share both successes and failures
   - Make data accessible and understandable

2. Participation Over Imposition
   - Co-create solutions with stakeholders
   - Provide meaningful choices, not just information
   - Act on feedback received (close the loop)

3. Quick Wins Build Momentum
   - Deliver visible improvements early
   - Celebrate successes publicly
   - Use wins to justify longer-term investments

4. Honesty About Trade-offs
   - Acknowledge when choices are difficult
   - Explain criteria for prioritization
   - Don't promise what can't be delivered

5. Consistency Builds Trust
   - Follow through on commitments
   - Maintain steady communication rhythm
   - Align actions with stated values
```
