- Historical rebellion against feudal obligations (1816-1824)
- Residents considered themselves "free citizens" (not serfs)
- **Modern Impact:** Foundation of high civic engagement and political participation
- **Manifestation:** 72.58% voter turnout (above national average)

**Ethnic Tensions:**
- **"Tutejsi" vs "Letnicy":** Locals vs. summer residents dispute over moral right to decide gmina's future

- **Current State:** Managed through inclusive cultural programs and transparent governance
