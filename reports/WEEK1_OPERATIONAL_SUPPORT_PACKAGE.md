## Escalation Protocol

**Level 1: Working Group Lead**
- Issue: Minor delays, resource conflicts
- Response time: Same day
- Resolution: Reallocate resources, adjust timeline slightly

**Level 2: Steering Committee Chair (Wójt)**
- Issue: Significant blockers, budget overruns, stakeholder resistance
- Response time: Within 24 hours
- Resolution: Strategic decision, additional resources, stakeholder engagement

**Level 3: External Support**
- Issue: Legal challenges, regulatory complications, major crises
- Response time: Immediate
- Resolution: Engage legal counsel, contact higher authorities, crisis management

**Escalation Triggers:**
- Deadline at risk (>2 days delay)
- Budget variance >10%
- Stakeholder complaint received
- Regulatory issue identified
- Team member unavailable unexpectedly
